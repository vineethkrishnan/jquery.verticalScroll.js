# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free environment for everyone who participates in this project, regardless of background or experience.

## Expected Behavior

Examples of positive behavior include:
- Using respectful and inclusive language
- Being considerate of differing viewpoints
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what benefits the project and community
- Treating others with empathy and professionalism

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling or abusive comments
- Sharing private information without consent
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional or collaborative setting

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for setting expectations, addressing unacceptable behavior, and taking fair and appropriate action when needed.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, and discussions.

## Reporting and Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to  
**mail@vineethkrishnan.in**.  
All reports will be reviewed and handled responsibly and in good faith.
